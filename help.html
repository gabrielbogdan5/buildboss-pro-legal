General
What is BuildBoss PRO? ⌄

BuildBoss PRO is a professional construction site management app. It lets you create Site Audits, Toolbox Talks, Daily Activity Briefings (DAB), and Incident Reports — all from your phone, with no account required.

Do I need to create an account? ⌄

No account required. BuildBoss PRO works entirely on your device. No sign-up, no login, no email needed. Just install and use.

Is BuildBoss PRO free? ⌄

Yes, BuildBoss PRO is free to use. All core features — audits, toolbox talks, DAB, and incident reports — are included at no cost.

How do I install the app? ⌄

BuildBoss PRO is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Android, tap "Add to Home Screen" from your browser menu. On iOS (Safari), tap the Share button then "Add to Home Screen".

You can also install it directly from the Google Play Store.

Data & Privacy
Where is my data stored? ⌄

All your data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. Your reports stay private and on your phone.

Will I lose my data if I clear the app? ⌄

If you use Clear All Data in Settings, or clear your browser/app storage, your saved reports will be deleted. Always export important reports as PDFs before clearing data.

Does the app collect any personal data? ⌄

No. BuildBoss PRO does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

PDF & Export
How do I export a report as a PDF? ⌄

Navigate to the final step of any report (step Export) and tap the Export PDF button. Your device will open the print dialog — select "Save as PDF" or share it directly.

Can I add my company name to the PDF? ⌄

Yes! Go to Settings → PDF Options → Company Name. Whatever you enter will appear in the PDF footer on all exported documents.

Can I remove the date from the PDF? ⌄

Yes. In Settings → PDF Options, toggle off Show Date on PDF. The date will no longer appear in headers and footers.

Offline Use
Does the app work without internet? ⌄

Yes, fully offline. Once installed, BuildBoss PRO works with no internet connection. All features — including creating and exporting reports — are available offline.

The app is showing an "offline" page — what do I do? ⌄

This can happen if the app hasn't fully cached yet. Make sure you've opened the app at least once with an internet connection. Then close and reopen it — it should load normally offline.

Incident Reports
What is RIDDOR and do I need to report? ⌄

RIDDOR (Reporting of Injuries, Diseases and Dangerous Occurrences Regulations 2013) requires certain workplace incidents to be reported to the UK Health and Safety Executive (HSE).

This includes fatalities, specified injuries, over-7-day incapacitation, and dangerous occurrences. Visit hse.gov.uk/riddor for full guidance.

What incident types can I report? ⌄

BuildBoss PRO supports four incident types:

Accident — Injury occurred
Near Miss — No injury, but could have
Dangerous Occurrence — Reportable event
Property Damage — Equipment or structure
