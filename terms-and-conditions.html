Description of the App
Section 2

BuildBoss PRO is a construction site management tool designed to assist with:

  • Site Audits — documenting inspection issues and risk levels
  • Toolbox Talks — preparing and recording safety briefings
  • Daily Activity Briefings (DAB) — structured pre-work briefings
  • Incident Reporting — recording accidents, near misses, and dangerous occurrences
  • PDF Export — generating downloadable reports for site records

The App operates entirely offline on your device. No internet connection is required for core functionality.
