The Story

BuildBoss PRO was built out of a simple frustration: existing tools were too slow, too complex, or required an internet connection in places where signal is unreliable.

Construction sites need fast, no-nonsense tools. Filling in a site audit shouldn't require an account, a subscription, or a WiFi connection. So we built exactly that — a powerful app that lives entirely on your device.

From a first prototype to a fully offline PWA with PDF export, DAB briefings, RIDDOR-aware incident reporting, and toolbox talk generation — BuildBoss PRO has grown into a complete site management toolkit.
